Hostel and Canteen

Hostel:
  • Dr Samuel George Institute of Engineering and Technology is providing separate hostel accommodation for boys and girls.
  • The objective of the Hostel is to provide suitable and comfortable accommodation for students. The two hostels are supervised by the experienced wardens and caretakers whose keen observation and guidance enables the staff to discharge their duties effectively, while continuously maintaining discipline among the residents. These hostels are provided with sophisticated EPABX system, which allows the students to converse with their parents. The college has provided a facilities like STD KIOSK, Xerox center and cooperative stores for the usage of students. Delicious and Notorious Veg. & Non. Veg. food under hygienic conditions is provided for students at Lunch & Dinner. The college has a canteen within the campus catering to the needs of the students and staff. All bakery items, Tiffin and meals are provided at reasonable prices The college has got its own Mineral Water Plant for the supply of Protected Water round the clock. The college is equipped with one 250 KVA & two 82.5 capacity generator of kirloskar make for uninterrupted power supply.

Library

  • The central library Of SGIT presently covers a total user area of 1264 sqm, with a seating capacity of 250. We are maintaing Digital Library, E-Library, and Circulation section, journals, News papers & Magazines, reprographic service, question papers. Reference books,projects reports. The total no. of Titles are 5252 and the no. of volumes are 35007. The books are classified and arranged according to the Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC) scheme and “OPEN ACCESS” system is followed. The library has computerized catalogue along with manual catalogue to find out the availability of books. Students are allowed to go to the racks to pick the required books for their issue through bar-coding system. Students are allowed to use the reference books in the library. Reading and Group discussion tables are provided in the second floor to carry out the academic activity along with the staff members. Digital Library is provided in the ground floor and the video lectures are available in the server along with 60 terminals for the usage of students. With Wi-fi connectivity in the campus, the students can access the video lectures from anywhere in the campus. We subscribed NPTL Video lecture from IIT, Chennai.
  • Timings:
    • Monday to Saturday: 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
    • Sunday and Holidays: 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM
Library Membership and Loan Privileges
  • Books shall be issued to the members of the teaching and non-teaching staff of the college on the recommendation of the Principal.
  • To get enrolled as a staff member, a person shall fill up and sign a registration form, which can be had free of cost at the library.
Book Cards or Tickets
  • Each teaching member will be given ten book cards which entitle him to borrow ten books.
  • Each non-teaching member will be given Two readers tickets which entitle him to borrow Two books.
  • The membership will be valid for a year and need be renewed.
  • In case the member wants to withdraw membership, he/her should return all books due from him and pays overdue charges if any.
Mode of Issue
  • Every member, including the members of the teaching, non-teaching staff shall be provided with borrowers’ tickets which must be produced every time books are borrowed.
  • The borrowers will be responsible for all the books borrowed on their tickets and for all fines payable against them. Members are not allowed to sub-lend the books to the others.
  • The books issued for a Semester must be returned after the semester.
  • Members shall see that books have been properly issued and return on their cards. It is difficult to rectify mistakes later on.
  • Any book, which is temporarily in great demand, shall be issued for such shorter period, as be necessary, or temporarily declared as a Reference book. Loans may at any time be terminated by the order of the Librarian.
