Director's Message

It gives me immense pleasure addressing the aspirants seeking admissions in Dr.Samuel George Institute of Engineering & Technology (SGIT) and would like to share the core mission of this institution, which is to impart value-based quality education. We, at SGIT, strive towards achieving this mission by providing world-class education to our students and preparing them to become the new-age leaders. About Us

Dr Samuel George Institute of Engineering and Technology (SGIET) was established in 1997 at Markapur, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh. It is the long-awaited dream for Dr Samuel George – founder of the college. He is an educationalist, humanist and visionary, and academician who taught in various American Universities. His desire to impart quality technical education, combined with his affection for his native place, has resulted in the establishment of this Institute. The main objective of the Institute is to give the best possible technical education with state-of-the-art facilities.

Vision

SGIT students who participate in the career centre’s educational and experiential offerings will not only graduate with a promising future. They will be equipped with the career management skills and knowledge necessary for navigating that future and making a difference in the world through innovative, purposeful leadership.

Mission

To provide career education, resources, and experiential opportunities to SGIT students across all departments so that they are positioned to launch and sustain satisfying and successful careers that make a meaningful contribution to society.

We will continuously improve how we educate and innovate to develop adaptive problem-solvers capable of tackling our greatest challenges.
